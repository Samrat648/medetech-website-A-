About Us

Our Product: AirSense Radar Mat

Designed to fit under any mattress or chair, the AirSense Radar Mat passively tracks vital signs and alerts caregivers in real-time—all without the need for wearables or wires.

  • Contactless tracking of heart rate, breathing, and motion
  • Instant mobile alerts to caregivers and family
  • No discomfort, perfect for long-term use
  • Easy installation and setup at home
  • Affordable, reliable, and made for Indian homes
